Welcome to RLC Region 7

We are proud to host the Regional Leadership Conference for FBLA Region 7. This premier event brings together the brightest future business leaders from across our region for competition, learning, and networking.

Competitive Events

Students compete in over 25 different business-related events, showcasing their skills and knowledge.

Leadership Development

Workshops and sessions designed to develop the next generation of business leaders.

Networking

Connect with peers, advisors, and business professionals from across the region.

Conference Workshops

Our carefully curated workshops are designed to enhance leadership skills, business acumen, and professional development for all attendees. Each session is led by industry experts and experienced educators.

Leadership Development

  • Professional LinkedIn Headshots

    Get professional headshots taken for your LinkedIn profile

  • Cookie Decorating Workshop

    Learn creative cookie decorating techniques and team building

  • Public Speaking Workshop

    Practice presentation skills and overcome speaking anxiety

  • Leadership Games & Activities

    Interactive team-building exercises and leadership challenges

Business Skills

  • Business Card Design

    Create professional business cards using design software

  • Personal Finance Basics

    Learn budgeting, saving, and managing personal finances

  • Mock Job Interviews

    Practice interviewing skills with local business professionals

  • Social Media for Business

    Learn how to use social media professionally and effectively

Technology & Innovation

  • Excel for Business

    Learn essential Excel skills for data analysis and presentations

  • PowerPoint Presentation Skills

    Create engaging and professional presentations

  • Google Workspace Tools

    Master Google Docs, Sheets, and collaborative tools

  • Digital Portfolio Creation

    Build an online portfolio to showcase your skills and projects

Career Preparation

  • Resume Writing Workshop

    Create a professional resume with personalized feedback

  • College Application Tips

    Get guidance on college applications and scholarship opportunities

  • Networking & Communication

    Learn how to network effectively and communicate professionally

  • Business Etiquette & Dress

    Master professional dress codes and business etiquette

Ready to Join Us?
